Quarterly Access Review - Microsoft Entra ID
Authorized users are identified in the active employee and contractor roster.
Managers reviewed user access to SharePoint CUI sites and approved continued access.
Privileged accounts were reviewed by the IT/Security Owner.
Service accounts and processes acting on behalf of authorized users are listed in the identity inventory.
Managed Windows devices authorized to connect are tracked in Intune compliance reports.
Access is limited to authorized users, approved processes, and managed devices through Entra ID conditional access and MFA.
